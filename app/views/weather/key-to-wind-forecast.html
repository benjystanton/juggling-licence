Key to wind forecast

Compass direction

The arrow shows the direction that the wind is blowing in. For example, an arrow pointing up shows a wind blowing from the south to the north.

Average wind speed

Wind speed strengthens and weakens over time. This number represents the average wind speed expected for this time.

Wind gusts

Wind gusts show how strong the wind speed could get at any point during this time.

Wind direction

The letter represents the direction that the wind is blowing from. For example, The letter S indicates that it is a wind that comes from the south.
